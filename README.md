## Overview

AutoFix connects **drivers** and **automotive workshops** through a modern ecosystem focused on speed and convenience:

* **Mobile App** that helps users find nearby workshops based on their location.
* **Fast appointment booking** directly with selected workshops.
* **Web System** that allows workshops to manage incoming appointments efficiently.

AutoFix is designed to reduce friction between customers and workshops, making service scheduling simple and reliable.

---

## Features

### Users (Mobile App)

* Find nearby automotive workshops in real time
* Browse available services
* Schedule appointments quickly and easily
* Register vehicles
* Track appointment status

###  Workshops (Web System)

* Manage and confirm appointments
* Configure services and availability
* View customer and vehicle information
* Receive and manage booking requests
